Adjusted Close Prices Comparison

Interpretation

This line chart shows the adjusted closing prices for BGRN and GRNB ETFs over time. The chart illustrates how each ETF's price has changed in relation to the other, highlighting periods of volatility and stability.

Discussion

BGRN shows greater fluctuations in price over the observed period compared to GRNB, indicating potentially higher volatility or different market reactions to underlying assets.

Sources

Adjusted closing prices were obtained from Yahoo Finance and visualized using Python's matplotlib library.

Cumulative Returns Comparison

Interpretation

This visualization compares the cumulative returns of the BGRN and GRNB ETFs. Cumulative returns are a measure of the total change in investment value over a period of time.

Discussion

Both ETFs experienced significant growth with some downturns, reflecting the impact of market conditions on green bond investments.

Annual Performance Comparison

Interpretation

The bar chart presents the average annual cumulative returns for the BGRN and GRNB ETFs, along with the market index. It provides a snapshot of their performance relative to each other by year.

Discussion

The market index appears to outperform both ETFs in certain years, suggesting a more in-depth analysis could be valuable to understand the factors driving these differences.
